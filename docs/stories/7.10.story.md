# Story 7.10: Australia Payroll Support - Future

## Status
Future Story (Depends on Stories 7.1, 7.2, 7.3)

## Story
**As a** HR manager in Australia,
**I want** Australia-specific payroll processing with Superannuation, PAYG, WorkCover, and Fair Work Awards,
**so that** I can process payroll accurately and efficiently while maintaining compliance with Australian labor laws and tax regulations.

## Acceptance Criteria
1. **Superannuation Support:** 9.5% employer contribution with proper calculation and reporting
2. **PAYG Withholding:** Income tax withholding with ATO-compliant calculations
3. **WorkCover Support:** Workers compensation with state-specific rates
4. **Payroll Tax:** State-based payroll tax calculations and reporting
5. **Fair Work Awards:** Modern award system with penalty rates and overtime
6. **ATO Integration:** Australian Tax Office compliance and reporting
7. **State-Specific Rules:** Different rules for NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA, TAS, NT, ACT
8. **Currency Support:** Australian Dollar (AUD) with proper formatting
9. **Tax Year Handling:** Australian financial year (July 1 - June 30)
10. **Compliance Reporting:** ATO and state-specific compliance reports

## Dependencies
- **Story 7.1:** Multi-Region Foundation (provides country and currency support)
- **Story 7.2:** Payroll Configuration System (provides configuration framework)
- **Story 7.3:** Payroll Calculation Engine (provides calculation framework)

## Dev Notes
This story implements Australia-specific payroll requirements building upon the multi-region foundation and configuration system.
