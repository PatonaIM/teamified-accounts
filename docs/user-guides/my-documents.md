# My Documents - User Guide

## Overview
My Documents is a centralized hub for managing all your onboarding and employment documents. Access it directly at `/documents` or through the onboarding wizard.

## Document Categories

### CVs
- **Purpose**: Upload your curriculum vitae or resume
- **Accepted formats**: PDF, DOC, DOCX
- **Max size**: 5MB
- **Tips**: Keep your CV up to date; upload a new version when you update your experience

### Identity Documents
- **Purpose**: Passport, national IDs, driver's license
- **Accepted formats**: PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG
- **Max size**: 5MB
- **Important**: Ensure documents are clear and readable

### Employment Documents
- **Purpose**: Contracts, offer letters, employment certificates
- **Accepted formats**: PDF, DOC, DOCX
- **Max size**: 10MB

### Education Documents
- **Purpose**: Degrees, transcripts, certificates
- **Accepted formats**: PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG
- **Max size**: 10MB

## Document Status

### Pending
Your document has been uploaded and is awaiting review by HR.

### Verified
Your document has been reviewed and approved. **Verified documents cannot be deleted** - contact HR if you need to make changes.

### Needs Changes
HR has requested changes to your document. Check the notes provided and upload a new version.

## Features

### Version History
Click "Version History" to view all previous versions of your documents. The most recent upload is marked as "Current".

### Multiple Uploads
You can upload multiple documents in each category. Additional uploads don't replace existing ones unless you upload a new version of the same document type.

### Download
Click the download icon to save a copy of your document locally.

### Delete
Documents can be deleted unless they're verified. Use the delete icon to remove unwanted uploads.

## Troubleshooting

### Upload Failed
- **Check file size**: Ensure your file doesn't exceed the maximum size for that category
- **Check file format**: Only approved formats are accepted
- **Try again**: Network issues can cause uploads to fail - refresh and retry

### Can't Delete Document
Verified documents cannot be deleted by candidates or EOR employees. This protects approved documents from accidental deletion. Contact HR if you need to update a verified document.

### Document Not Appearing
- **Refresh the page**: Sometimes documents take a moment to appear
- **Check the correct tab**: Make sure you're viewing the right category
- **Contact support**: If issues persist, reach out to your HR administrator

## Best Practices

1. **Upload promptly**: Complete your document uploads as soon as possible during onboarding
2. **Use clear scans**: Ensure identity and education documents are legible
3. **Keep updated**: Upload new versions when your information changes
4. **Check for notes**: If a document needs changes, read the HR notes carefully before re-uploading

## Privacy & Security

- All documents are securely stored and encrypted
- Only authorized HR personnel can access your documents
- Document access is logged for audit purposes
- You can only view and manage your own documents

## Getting Help

If you encounter issues or have questions:
1. Check this guide for common solutions
2. Contact your HR administrator via email or the support portal
3. Include specific error messages if reporting an issue
